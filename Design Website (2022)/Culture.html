A Brief Outline of Pakistan Culture













Culture & Tranditions

Pakistan is a highly reilgious country, 96.2 percent of Pakistan's population are Muslims. Meaning their hoildays or dates of importance would also be largely related to Islam. The following dates of importance follow the Islamic lunar calendar, meaning that these dates cannot be applied to the Gregorian calendar.

Al-Hijra
This is the Islamic calendar's equivalent to New Years day. This day was the end of their prophet's (Mohammad) journey.

Eid ul-Adha
A 4 day hoilday marking the sacrifices of Abraham, a prophet and the first Hebrew patriarch.

Eid ul-Fitr
A 3 day hoilday invloving prayers and feasts in the evening. Marks the end of a month of fasting.

Prophet's Birthday
The Prophet Muhammad's birthday, often celebrated with communal meals.

Ramadan
The start of a month of fasting. An entire month where Muslims disallow themselves from eating (occasionally drinking as well), for certian periods of time.

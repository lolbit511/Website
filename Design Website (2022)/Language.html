A Brief Outline of Pakistan Culture













Symbols

Explanations

There are many different symbols and objects that have great importance in Pakistan culture, this ranges from flowers to animals to reilgious symbols.

A jasmine flower is known for its smell and apperance, this flower symbolizes amiability and modesty.

A cotton plant is an important part of Pakistan culture, not just in symbolization, but also in its active economy. Pakistan is one of the largest cotton producers in the world, and cotton plants have also been nicknamed "white gold" for this purpose.

Islam is Pakistan's main reiligion, so it should be no suprise that the star and cresent symbol that once represented Islam also holds importance in Pakistan nowadays. an important thing to note is that this is not the offical symbol of Islam, the symbol of Islam has taken many forms, and the star and cresent is simply the most regonizable amongst them all.

The Shaheen Falcon is the state bird of Pakistan. It is a symbolic icon that also represents the Pakistani Air Force.

The Chukar partridge is the national bird of Pakistan, it symbolizes intense love, often unreturned love.

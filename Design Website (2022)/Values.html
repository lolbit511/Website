Values

Honour: As with most Middle Eastern countries, honour amongst family and other relationships. Honour is also known as "Izzat" in Pakistan, their Izzat is influcenced and changed by their actions and the actions of people who are close to them. Meaning if someone does something that brings them shame, they are also directly impacting the honor of their community.

Patriotism: Patriotism in Pakistan is linked to how honor is treated. Pakistanis see themselves as a community, it should no suprise that honor for their country is highly valued.

Generosity: Generosity is shown almost everywhere in Pakistan. Most shopkeepers allow shopers to bargin for prices far lower than the quoted. Hospitality in the form of invitations to their homes can be common.


Respect: Respect is highly linked with etiquette in Pakistan, inquiring about other's wellfare and contentment is common, failing to do so may make you appear lacking in sentivity.

Dignity: Pakistan values human diginity seriously. Laws regarding human diginity and rights are strict.

Collectivism: Pakistan values collectivism very much, as mentioned above, they see themselves as a community.

Faith: Faith is seen as the bond and link between people, the ability to have faith and to show faith to others signifies their closeness.
